About Me

Hey, it's me Syazwan! I am a third year Electrical Engineering undergraduate in Universiti Malaya. I was born and raised in Penang. I am an organized individual with a huge passion in coding.

I have worked on an integrated design project (IDP) during my third year studies titled Mobile Smoke Tracking System. This project aims to create an integration between unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV) and sensors array to detect methane leakage. I also have worked on a Flight Booking System web application using python modules like Flask and SQLAlchemy.

Currently, I am working on final year project (FYP) titled: IOT based Multisensors Integration for Fugitive Gas Localization
